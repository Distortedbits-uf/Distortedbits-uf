About

Welcome to Distortedbits

Distortedbits UF is a company consisting of four technology enthusiastic students from NTI Stockholm studying business or "Entreprenörskap" as part of our individual choice in year 2 of gymnasium in Stockholm Sweden. We are currently developing and producing a USB cable that can prevent data transfers from a mobile device to a usb port.

We are a UF company

Distortedbits is a UF company, meaning that we are an educational company. UF stems from Ungforetagsamhet, meaning youthful entrepreneurship, which is a part of J.A Worldwide. More information about UF can be found at their site (https://ungforetagsamhet.se/), and you can find us at our UF page (https://ungforetagsamhet.se/company/ distortedbits-uf)

Our Goal and Vision

Our goal is to create a product that makes it easier for everyday people to feel secure when charging their mobile phones through public USB sockets. Today there are several solutions that can be purchased through the internet, but these are often meant for hobbyists and enthusiasts, and therefore uses inappropriate language and confusing technicalities terms. Our vision is that even the everyday person should be able to take part in what is usually locked behind confusing wording, so that everyone can feel safe in public spaces.

You can read our entire business plan at: Affärsplan.pdf

Our Idea

We will sell a USB cable whose data transfer can be turned off with the push of a button, this is because people should feel safer when charging mobile or the like through public USB ports.

Sustainability

First and foremost, we want to say that we place great emphasis on the sustainability of our company, both in terms of sustainable business and sustainable development. Therefore, we will work with sustainability in an ecological, economic, ethical, and social perspective. We also work to ensure that the work environment is sustainable.
